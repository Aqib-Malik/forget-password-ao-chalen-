# Password Reset Instructions

## How to Reset Your Password

If you have forgotten your password for the Ride Share App, please follow these steps:

### Step 1: Contact Support
Since password reset requires email verification, please contact our support team to reset your password.

### Option A: Email Support
Send an email to: **hmaqib91@gmail.com**
- Subject: Password Reset Request
- Include your registered phone number in the email
- Our support team will help you.


### Step 3: Login with New Password
1. Open the Ride Share App
2. Enter your phone number
3. Enter your new password
4. Tap "Login"

## Important Notes

- **Email Required**: To use the password reset feature, you must have added an email address to your account. If you haven't added an email yet, please go to your Profile screen and add your email address first.

- **Security**: For security reasons, password reset requests are processed manually by our support team to ensure account security.

- **Response Time**: Our support team typically responds within 24 hours during business days.

- **Phone Number**: Make sure to provide the exact phone number you used during registration.
